Why everybody likes tea?

Tea is a useful product, but nobody cares about that. Tea is a drink that provokes people to long and warm communication. It makes the atmosphere more comfortable and relaxing.

A cup of fragrant drink during a break is an opportunity to relax and relieve stress.

It is difficult to find a person who do not find the taste of tea special. In addition, the choice of tea is huge. Tea can be black, green, white or enriched with aromatic additives.

Tea occupies a special place in human’s life. Unlikely that the situation will change soon, and it is wonderful. Life without tea would be less vivid and rich.

2 types of tea

There are 2 packaging types for finished tea - packaged in little bags and loose leaf tea. Each type has its own pros and cons, but always finds its buyer. The choice of tea packaging depends on your lifestyle and own values.

Let's consider the difference between packaged and loose leaf tea.

  • Pros of tea bags:
  • Leaves do not fall into the cup
  • It is easy to transport
  • Simplicity and high brewing speed
  • A wide range of product
  • Low price
  • Cons:
  • Tea bags may contain harmful addivities
  • Low content of beneficial properties
  • Not high saturation
  • Pros of loose leaf tea:
  • High quality
  • Large selection
  • Real health benefits
  • Nature flavor
  • Cons:
  • Not convenient to transport
  • The complexity and duration of cooking
  • High price
